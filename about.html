About Us

Wazitech Business Solutions offers expert photography, videography, and branding for companies, organizations, and churches. We blend creativity, skill, and technology to deliver impactful visuals, from weddings to corporate events, with a touch of African authenticity.

Why Choose Us

At Wazitech Business Solutions, we go beyond delivering media—we build lasting connections. We take the time to understand your story, vision, and values, ensuring every photo and video truly reflects who you are. With a creative lens rooted in African identity and backed by professionalism, we capture moments with care, purpose, and heart. Partner with us, and become a collaborator in creating something timeless.
